Emphasis and strengthened text

This is a paragraph that has strong text, and emphasized text. I'm going to talk about how my UX205 Midterm went. To start off, with all my Midterms, I go in with an open and calm mind, and try not to freak out as much in fear that I will forget what I learned. What I was not expecting was the fact that there were a lot of short answer questions that took up most of my time! I have not written a test like that in years; where your hand actually starts to hurt. I feel confident that I did decent on the Midterm, and now I know that Dr. Goodrum's Midterms require a lot of writing!

The reason why it is better to use strong and emphasis rather than i and b tags because strong and emphasis actually effect the content of the paragraph opposed to just changing the style of the text. It actually signifies that text is supposed to be more important than the rest of the text, whereas i and b tags just present the text differently.
